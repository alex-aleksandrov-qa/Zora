Welcome to Zora - Shkorpilovtsi holiday village

Where, if not here?
The sea is waiting for you.

Located in the picturesque resort village of Shkorpilovtsi, just a few minutes walk from the longest sandy beach on the Black Sea coast, Zora Villa Village offers you the ideal combination of tranquility, nature and comfort.
Our cozy villas are suitable for families, couples and groups of friends. Each villa has:

  • Parking space for your car
  • Air conditioning and TV
  • Free Wi-Fi
